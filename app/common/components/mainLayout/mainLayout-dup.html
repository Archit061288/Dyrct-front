What is DYRCT?

DYRCT is a destination and location management platform making address sharing, navigation and trip management simple, fast and accurate.

From sharing a single location with an individual or group to sharing multi-location folders to your entire contact list, Dyrct is effective, easy to use and will simplify your life.

Dyrct is a Mobile and Web supported network of user-managed online Connected Identities (CiD) which are easily created, saved and shared among users. Privacy and viewing restrictions can be altered for your entire contact list or customised for individual contacts.

This CiD forms the basis of common-destination user groups and multi-CiD themed folders both of which have wide social networking, travel and business applications.

Our ‘one-touch’ navigation launch interface avoids the complexity & input inaccuracy typical of traditional navigation devices.

SOME KEY FEATURES

Meeting a friend at a new restaurant, or organizing a social event?

Our address sharing and group invitation functions are what you need. And don’t just share your holiday photo’s – share your destination folder as well.

Navigating to a new client or helping them find you?

In your own city or far away this can be complex and time-consuming. With Dyrct you can send and receive accurate addresses, manage multiple folders and keep an accurate logbook record of every trip.

Going somewhere?

Save an existing Point of Interest or create your own. Save and share any location from a City Hotel to a farm-gate on a deserted road.

CID

Your Connected Identity (CiD) is the heart of your Dyrct experience.
It includes some basic information and a chosen fixed location for you or your business. The CiD is user-created so it is personalized, editable and accurate.

Navigating

With simple address sharing and one-touch navigation, Dyrct is a powerful navigation tool.
Imagine accurate and simple navigating from your mobile device using your preferred mapping software? This is it.

Sharing

Are you still manually sending address details, downloading maps and dropping pins?
Don’t be that person. With Dyrct, simply share your location and Points of Interest with just one swipe.

Groups

You’re planning a lunch or a meeting or just getting together? Or perhaps you need to arrange where to meet the fan-bus at the stadium.
It’s so easy. Decide who you want in the group, add the place you are meeting and send. Now everyone just has to click once on the venue and they are on their way! No giving directions and no-one is going to get lost.

Folders

You’ve just taken the trip of a lifetime to an exotic foreign location. You know all the hotspots and experienced great hotels and restaurants. You know you just have to share these with your friends.
Simple! Create a folder, pull in the CiD of every place you visited and share the whole trip!. Send it to your friend or perhaps … send it everyone!

Logbook

Remembering where you went today, or yesterday or last week is just beyond you? Well we’re here to help. Every trip taken is saved and you can manage multiple logbooks.
Now just send these to that person asking where you were yesterday.
