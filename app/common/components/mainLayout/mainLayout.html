What is DYRCT?

With DYRCT, destination sharing, address finding, navigation and trip management is simple, fast, effective and accurate.

DYRCT is a mobile, web and cloud-supported platform, supporting a community of user-managed Connected Identities & Points Of Interest which are easily created, saved and shared - opening the way for unlimited social and business applications.

From the simplicty and ease-of-use of our ‘one-touch’ navigation interface to the versatility of our Folders, DYRCT will simplify your life.

 General User Tips
  • Your user profile is visible to all your contacts and will also be visible when they share it with others. To protect your privacy it is only necessary to add your name to your profile. Optional settings allow you to add your home or work address as well as other information - these details will then also be visible. This is particularly useful if you want other users to easily navigate to you.
  • Be sure to end your trips after you have finished them – this must be done in the APP (you probably left your App open on the navigation launch screen). Ending a trip allows an accurate trip record to be created and exported.
  • Dyrct imports your contacts from your mobile device - with your permission of course. However you will still need to INVITE those contacts to join Dyrct if they are not already on Dyrct. This can be done in SETTINGS.
  • Folders are incredibly useful and we know you will create many of them and share them with everyone. We suggest taking care when naming them so you can archive them easily. Folder names are editable.
  • If you are creating multiple Address or many Folders we suggest you do this from the website as it may be easier and quicker.
  • You can invite a contact to a specific location without making a Address by simply selecting INVITE alongside their name. This allows you to create a meeting location to send to that contact. The default is your current location.
